Compared to the conceptual design, my website went through quite a few iterations. The original plan was a comprehensive, searchable resource for tech products aimed at students. Due to professor feedback that my target audience was too broad, I decided to reduce the scope to focus specifically on products that will be helpful for design students at Laurier.

The website was originally split into different pages for each product section, but as I narrowed the focus and reduced the number of products (to only the best recommendations) I decided the pages added more navigational complexity than value so I combined all sections into a single, easy-to-read page. Search functionality also proved to be less useful so it was removed.
